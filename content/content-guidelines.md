## What is allowed?
#### Almost anything, really...
1. You may use my characters for whatever creative projects you want, both non-profit and for profit. This includes, but is not limited to, fanart, cosplay, merchandising (stickers, keychains, etc). Regardless of wether the project is non-profit or for profit, you must state clearly that it is derivative, fan-made work based upon my source material.
2. You may reproduce artworks created by me (@flatpancakesss) in any non-profit scenario, such as profile pictures, anime image boards, etc. Credit given highly appreciated, though not necessary. These terms only apply to artworks created by me specifically, for official artworks created by other artists please confirm their terms with them directly.
3. You may use recorded gameplay footage from my games, including narrative sequences, on video sharing platforms such as YouTube or Twitch in any format you like. Gameplay videos, walkthroughs, livestreams, etc. It's all fine by me. 


## What is not allowed?

1. Content that is harmful to others and/or political in nature. Racism, sexism, extremeist ideologies, or any other generally distasteful things. As long as you have some common decency you'll be fine.
2. Claming ownership of my characters. You may **NOT** reproduce my art in any scenario where profit is involved. 
3. Reposting of any soundtracks featured within [the game]. The music was composed and created by STEEL PLUS and the rights to the music belong to him. 
4. You may not use any of my works to train generative AI models and you may not use generative AI models to generate content containing/based on/resembling anything related to my works.
5. You may not redistribute my games nor offer alternative download links seperate from the official download sites. This is to hold everybody's safety within best interests, as unofficial download links may be hosted by bad actors to distribute malware.
